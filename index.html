Battle with the OG's of action cinema. To play, first choose the character you want to be. Then, choose the opponent you want to face. When your ready to begin click attack. Players start with a certain amount of health and deliver each attack with a different amount of power. With each attack, players health decreases. As you gain experience watch your players attacks increase in power!
If you can defeat all the other players you win!
